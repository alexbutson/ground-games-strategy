Impact Realization Capability Assessment

Know exactly where you stand — before you invest in change.

ORCA is a structured diagnostic that tells you which of three forces — capability, capacity, or culture — is the binding constraint on your ability to execute strategy right now. It points to the single intervention that will unlock the most value first.

Book an ORCA Assessment What it measures

Three dimensions

Capability. Capacity. Culture.

Any one of the three can stall an entire strategy. ORCA diagnoses all three so you intervene where it counts.

Capability

Do your people have the skills to execute? ORCA surfaces where capability gaps quietly cap performance.

Capacity

Do they have the bandwidth to focus? ORCA measures how much reactive work is displacing planned, high-value work.

Culture

Does the environment reinforce the outcomes you want? ORCA reads whether behaviors support — or quietly resist — alignment.

Two formats

Self-assessment or expert-led

Format A

Self-Assessment

A structured instrument your leadership team completes independently.

  • Guided questionnaire across all three dimensions
  • Automated scoring & constraint profile
  • Recommended starting intervention
  • Ideal for a fast internal baseline
