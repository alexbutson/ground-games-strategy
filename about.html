Who we are

We exist to close the gap between strategy and results.

Ground Games Strategy is a consulting and capability-building firm. We help senior leaders build the structural conditions that make execution predictable — and then we teach their organizations to sustain it without us.

Our thesis

Strategy is a people problem dressed up as a process problem

Process can be designed. Technology can be deployed. Structure can be drawn on a chart. But until you build a self-reinforcing system that aligns people, measurement, and accountability to the outcomes that matter, you are managing symptoms — not causes.

That conviction is the foundation of everything we do: the Strategic Impact Realization Framework and a workshop curriculum built to be delivered by your own people.

What we believe

  • People are not the obstacle to strategy — they are the medium through which it lives or dies.
  • Most underperformance is frozen value, not a resource gap.
  • Alignment is a practice you sustain, not a state you reach.
  • Capability you own beats capability you rent.

The framework behind the firm

Ground Games Strategy is built on the Strategic Impact Realization Framework, developed by Adam Asch over years of work helping organizations turn strategic intent into measurable, sustainable impact. The framework is the subject of his forthcoming book, Impact by Design: How Organizations Stop Chasing Results and Start Realizing Them.

Our vision is simple and ambitious: to make SIRF a recognized standard for strategy realization — and to put it in the hands of a growing community of certified practitioners and trainers.
