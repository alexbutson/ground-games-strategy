The Workshops

Four workshops, one delivery system

Each can run on its own or as a sequence. Together they install the full Impact Delivery System.

Workshop 01

Strategy Creation

Build a strategy map that functions as a navigation tool, not a slide. Leaders leave able to express their strategy as cause and effect — what to achieve, in what order, through what mechanisms, with what dependencies.

  • Strategy mapping & cause-effect logic
  • Defining strategic value & priorities
  • The deliberate "yes": testing new commitments against fit and capacity
Workshop 02

Strategy Execution

Run the five-phase SIRE methodology to cascade enterprise strategy into functional delivery — without losing coherence or momentum between the boardroom and the front line.

  • Functional contribution mapping
  • Cross-functional dependency management
  • Initiative prioritization & governance cadence
Workshop 03

IVI — Intent, Value & Impact

Install the translation layer between strategy and activity. Every team leader leaves able to state, in one sentence, how their work advances the organization's top priority.

  • Individual & functional intent
  • Connecting contribution to outcomes
  • Measuring impact, not activity
Workshop 04

LAMP — Leadership & Management Performance

Make alignment a practice leaders sustain. Separate the roles — executives set direction and remove barriers; managers translate strategy into daily decisions — and the behaviors that keep both honest.

  • Executive vs. management alignment roles
  • Leadership consistency & accountability
  • Sustaining alignment between review cycles

Train-the-Trainer

Build internal capability that outlasts the engagement

Consulting creates a moment of change. Certified trainers create a lasting capability. Our train-the-trainer pathway licenses your people to deliver the SIRF workshops with full fidelity — turning a one-time program into a permanent operating discipline.

  • Accredited curriculum, facilitation guides, and participant materials
  • Co-delivery and supervised practice before solo certification
  • Ongoing access to updates, community, and master faculty

Why organizations certify their own

A recognized internal faculty means consistent language, repeatable results, and a strategy-execution capability you own — not one you rent. It is the fastest route to making impact realization part of how the organization simply works.
